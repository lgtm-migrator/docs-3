Most of our platform are released under the [Apache 2.0 license][apache-2.0]. We
love Open Source and as Vercel, we also see Open Source as our medium.

We are always looking for help with our projects. Read our [contribution
guide][contribute] to get started!

## Projects

Below you'll find our Open Source projects and the intentions our team has with
them.

### [tabetalt/backoffice][]

![tabetalt/backoffice](/img/repositories/backoffice.jpg)

_Backoffice is the administration console for Tabetalt_

Tabetalt Backoffice is a React-based administration console for the whole
e-commerce solution.

[Check it out on Github][tabetalt/backoffice]

### [tabetalt/kit][]

![tabetalt/kit](/img/repositories/kit.jpg)

**The technical implementation of Tabetalt Designsystem, based on Theme UI.**

Tabetalt Kit is the technical implementation of Tabetalt Designsystem. It is
based on Theme UI and features theme and a collection of components for use in
Tabetalt applications.

Our intentions are to stylize according to Tabetalt Designsystem on all Theme UI
components.

[Check it out on Github][tabetalt/kit]
